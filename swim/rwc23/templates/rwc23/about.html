About

  • It's a competition to predict the scores of the Rugby World Cup 2023 games.
  • There is a separate 'competition' for each week of the round robin part of Rugby World Cup, and for the quarter finals, semi-finals and finals.
  • You need to try and predict the winner and by how much for every game in the round.
  • It's a bit of fun.

Rules

  1. You must be an open-water swimmer on Auckland North shore, or be associated with one. Basically if you know Jo, Alton, Jim, Geoff or Helen you are in.
  2. The game will be a series of mini-competitions, one a week. The first 5 are weeks 1 - 5 of the round robin competition. The next 3 will be the quarter finals, semi finals & then the final.
  3. $5 to enter each week. Based on feedback we may increase the entry to $10 for the last few weeks.
  4. Predictions must be provided on this website or via via the Open Water swimming social WhatsApp group.
  5. Entries must be received before the start of each game.
  6. People who have paid (or pledged their entry fee), but forget to post their predicted score, or choose not to post a predicted score, will be assigned 0 points.
  7. Jo is the treasurer and will collect entry payments & pay out any prizes.

Scoring

  1. You must pick the winning team, or a draw, to get any points for that game.
  2. If you don't get your pick in before the game starts you don't get any points
  3. If you pick a draw (and get t right) you get 25 points (bonus for being brave).
  4. Your score is then based on points differential. The closer your selection is to the game score the more points you get.

Prizes

  • All entries for each round will be paid to the winner of the round.
  • The prize for each round will be split if there is a tie for 1st place.

Judging

  • Jim West and Jo ‘Frau’ Hammer are the administrators, and nominally the judges, and their decision is final.
